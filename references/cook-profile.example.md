# Cook Profile

Your personal cooking profile. The skills read this to tailor guidance to your equipment, experience, and preferences.

Copy this file to `cook-profile.md` and fill in your details:
```
cp references/cook-profile.example.md references/cook-profile.md
```

## Background
- Your cooking experience level and background
- Areas of strength and areas you're developing
- What you want to get out of guided cooking sessions

## Equipment

### Heat Sources
- Oven (type)
- Stovetop (gas/electric/induction)
- Other (air fryer, sous vide, grill, smoker, etc.)

### Cookware
- Dutch oven(s) — size and material
- Frying pans — sizes
- Pots — sizes
- Sheet pans
- Specialty items

### Temperature Instruments
- Thermocouple / probe thermometer — model, response time, calibration status
- IR thermometer — calibration status
- See `calibration.md` for offset data

### Prep & Processing
- Stand mixer, food processor, blender, etc.
- Kitchen scale (essential for protocols)
- Knives and cutting boards
- Note any equipment you do NOT have — the skills will suggest alternatives

## Preferences
- Your approach (fast weeknight vs. best-possible results)
- Seasoning habits (salt early? salt late?)
- Science curiosity level (just tell me what to do vs. explain everything)
- Dietary restrictions or preferences

## Household
- Typical number of servings
- Any dietary restrictions for the household

## Kitchen Environment
- Altitude (affects boiling point — sea level is 100C, 1000m is ~97C)
- Water hardness (affects seasoning)
- Any other environmental notes

## Notes
- Allergies, intolerances, or foods to avoid
- Anything else the skills should know about
